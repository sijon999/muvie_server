Payments & Subscription 3

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as payment services like PayPal and Apple Pay. You can also use gift cards or promotional codes, if available.

  • Are in-app purchases refundable?

    In-app purchases are generally non-refundable, but we may make exceptions in certain circumstances, such as if there was a technical issue or if the purchase was made by mistake. Please contact our support team if you believe you are eligible for a refund.

  • How do I cancel a subscription?

    You can cancel a subscription at any time by going to your account settings and selecting the option to cancel. Please note that cancelling a subscription does not result in a refund for any remaining time left in the current billing cycle.

Privacy & Secruity 3

  • What information do you collect from users?

    We only collect the information necessary to provide our service to you, such as your name, email address, and payment information. We do not sell or share your information with third parties, except as required by law or to provide our service to you.

  • How do you protect my personal information?

    We take the security of your personal information seriously and use industry-standard security measures to protect it. This includes encrypting sensitive data, limiting access to authorized personnel only, and regularly monitoring our systems for vulnerabilities.

  • Can I delete my account and personal information?

    Yes, you can delete your account and personal information at any time by contacting our support team. Please note that some information may be retained for legal or business reasons, such as transaction records or backup copies of data.
